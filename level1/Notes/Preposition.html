4. Types of Prepositions (Very Important)

4.1 Prepositions of Place

These show where something is.

Common Prepositions:
in, on, at, under, over, behind, in front of, between, among, inside, outside

The keys are on the table.
The dog is under the bed.
She is standing between two cars.

4.2 Prepositions of Time

IN

Used for longer periods of time.

  • in January
  • in 2025
  • in the morning
I was born in 2004.

ON

Used for specific days and dates.

  • on Monday
  • on 15th August
We have a test on Friday.

AT

Used for exact time.

  • at 6 pm
  • at midnight
The class starts at 9 o‚Äôclock.

4.3 Prepositions of Movement

These show movement or direction.

Examples: to, into, onto, from, through, across, towards

She is going to school.
The cat jumped onto the table.
He walked through the park.

4.4 Prepositions of Manner / Instrument

She wrote the letter with a pen.
He came by bus.
She left in a hurry.

4.5 Prepositions of Reason / Purpose

He was punished for his mistake.
The match was cancelled because of rain.
